# RiftbornAI Cinematics School

**Read this track before creating or editing Level Sequences, cinematic cameras, shot tracks, or render-output passes.**

Cinematics are not just moving cameras. They are shot-driven storytelling:

- they tell the player where to look
- they control emphasis, reveal, and rhythm
- they shape the emotional reading of a scene
- they must hold up before the final render, not only after it

Sequencer is a powerful system, but good shots still start from purpose and composition.

## Core Rules

- Every shot needs a job.
- Camera motion should support the scene, not compete with it.
- Bind real actors and confirm the bindings in Sequencer.
- Review shots before rendering them.
- Treat final render as proof of a finished sequence, not as the first iteration step.
